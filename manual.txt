5. What the Program Checks
- Identifies students with marks < 12.
- Validates email addresses using regex:
  ^[A-Za-z0-9._%+-]+@[A-Za-z0-9.-]+\.[A-Za-z]{2,}$
- Generates report.txt with the final results.
